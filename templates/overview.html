About This Tool

What Choices Shape What AI Finds?

AI is shaping real decisions — but the choices behind these systems aren't visible. Builders can't easily show them, decision makers can't assess them, and those affected can't question them. AtlasED analyses thousands of policy documents to show how design choices shape what AI finds.

Why This Matters A policymaker asks an AI model what the biggest issue in education is. The model says "learning outcomes," so funding follows. Change one setting, and that single category separates into distinct measures — reading scores, maths attainment, progress rates, and early years development — each requiring different interventions. The result wasn't wrong, it depended on how the problem was defined.

Our Model and What We Varied

Core Model

Production model chosen through standard checks. Uses Non-negative Matrix Factorisation (NMF) — a transparent method where every setting is visible and auditable.

What We Varied
Data composition
Education media makes up ~70% of documents. We rerun the model without it to test impact.
Number of topics
We compare 30 topics with simpler (5) and more detailed (15) versions.
Key Point

These are just a few of many possible choices. Every choice shapes results — and every choice can be tested.
