Core Finding The same question produces different answers depending on which documents the AI uses. With media included, the focus is on crises - funding, Ofsted, strikes. Without it, the focus shifts to structural issues - SEND, system fragmentation, post-16 barriers. Both are valid, but neither is complete. The difference comes from which voices are included.
Core Insight Media connects different parts of the system but also frames issues as crises. Without it, perspectives fragment but reveal deeper structural problems. The question isn't which version is right, but which one is shaping decisions - and whether decision-makers know.
